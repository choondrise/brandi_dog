# Brändi Dog Game Rules

## 1. Players and Teams

- The game has 4 players.
- Players are split into 2 teams of 2.
- Teammates sit opposite each other.
- Each player controls 4 pawns.

## 2. Objective

- A team wins when all 8 pawns belonging to that team are in their safe zones.
- A player cannot finish the game using a Joker.
- A Joker may be used to bring the first 3 pawns into the safe zone, but not the 4th/final pawn.

## 3. Board Structure

- The board has a circular main track.
- Each player has:
  - a start area,
  - an entry field,
  - a section of the main track,
  - a safe zone / finish zone.

## 4. Card Dealing

- Card rounds follow this dealing cycle:
  - 6 cards
  - 5 cards
  - 4 cards
  - 3 cards
  - 2 cards
- After the normal dealing cycle, the game continues with dice-based rounds.
- In dice-based rounds, the number of cards is determined by dice.

## 5. Card Exchange

- Before playing a hand, each player must exchange exactly 1 card with their teammate.
- Other communication between teammates is not allowed.

## 6. Hidden Information

- Players can see the board state.
- Players cannot see the other players’ cards.

## 7. General Movement

- Pawns move according to the value and effect of the played card.
- A move is legal only if the full path is legal.
- Pawns move on the main track unless they legally enter their safe zone.
- A pawn may enter its safe zone only if the safe-zone entry rule is satisfied.

## 8. Capturing

- Landing on an opponent’s pawn captures it.
- A captured pawn is sent back to its start area.

## 9. Entry Fields

- Passing over an entry field is not allowed.
- An occupied entry field blocks movement for all players.

## 10. Backward Movement

- A player may move backwards from any position on the main track.
- Moving backwards from the entry field is allowed.
- Example: moving backwards 4 from entry field `0` is legal.

## 11. Safe-Zone Entry

- A pawn may enter its safe zone only from the correct track end from that player’s perspective.
- A pawn cannot enter the safe zone by moving forward first and then immediately moving backwards into the safe zone.
- Example of illegal movement:
  - Move forward by `1`.
  - Then use backward movement to enter the safe zone directly.
- To enter the safe zone, the pawn must approach it from the proper end of the main track.

## 12. Card 4

- A `4` may be used to move either:
  - `+4` forward
  - `-4` backward

## 13. Card 7

- A `7` must move exactly 7 total spaces.
- The 7 may be split across multiple own pawns.
- The 7 may also be split across teammate pawns when applicable.
- The full value of 7 must be used.
- A 7 may enter the safe zone if the safe-zone entry rule is satisfied.
- Pawns passed over or landed on during a 7 movement are captured according to the game rules.

## 14. Jack

- Jack swaps two pawns.
- Jack may only swap pawns that are on the main track.
- Jack cannot be used for:
  - entry moves
  - pawns in start areas
  - pawns in safe zones
  - colored/finish fields

## 15. Joker

- Joker copies the selected card.
- Joker inherits all rules, restrictions, and effects of the copied card.
- Joker cannot be used to finish the game.
- A player may use a Joker to bring their first 3 pawns into the safe zone.
- A player may not use a Joker to bring their 4th/final pawn into the safe zone.
